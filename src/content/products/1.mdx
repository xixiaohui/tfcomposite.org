## Product Overview

**FRP Grating** (Fiberglass Reinforced Plastic Grating) is a strong, lightweight, corrosion-resistant structural panel made from a matrix of fiberglass and resin. It is commonly used in chemical plants, marine applications, walkways, and platforms.

### Key Features

- ✔️ Excellent corrosion resistance
- ✔️ High strength-to-weight ratio
- ✔️ Fire-retardant options available
- ✔️ Non-slip surface
- ✔️ Long service life with low maintenance
